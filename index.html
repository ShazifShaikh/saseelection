Our Mission:

SASE is dedicated to the advancement of Asian heritage scientists and engineers in education and employment so that they can achieve their full career potential. In addition to professional development, SASE also encourages members to contribute to the enhancement of the communities in which they live.

Prepare

Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success in the global business world.

Provide

opportunities for members to make contributions to their local communities.

Celebrate

diversity on campuses and in the workplace.

For more information about SASE, visit saseconnect.org

Syracuse University Chapter:

The Syracuse Chapter of SASE was officially founded in Fall 2011 by a group of young engineers who wanted to expand the number of professional Asian interest organizations on the Syracuse University campus. Our mission as the SASE Syracuse chapter is to be a professional Asian-interest (but not exclusive) organization that promotes diversity through community service, academics, and mentoring young students. Our chapter aims to provide our members with opportunities to advance their career aspirations through workshops, seminars, and networking.
